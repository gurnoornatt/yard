Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

When you book a discovery call, we collect your name, email address, and any information you provide in the booking form (firm name, target market, deal size, notes). We use this solely to conduct the call and follow up on your inquiry.

We use RB2B, a visitor identification service, to identify companies visiting this site. RB2B may associate your IP address with publicly available business information. No personally identifiable browsing data is stored on our servers.

How we use it

Your information is used only to respond to your inquiry and, if you opt in, to send occasional updates about our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

  • Cal.com — manages booking scheduling. Subject to Cal.com's privacy policy.
  • RB2B — company-level visitor identification. Subject to RB2B's privacy policy.
  • Vercel — hosts this website. Standard web server logs (IP, browser, page) may be retained per Vercel's policy.

Data retention

Booking and inquiry data is retained for as long as the business relationship is active, and deleted upon request. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.
